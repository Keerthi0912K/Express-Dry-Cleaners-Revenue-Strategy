Real fieldwork engagement with a 30-year-old family dry cleaning business in Hyderabad. Collected 3 months of transaction data on-site, structured it, and delivered strategy recommendations that were actually implemented.

## The business
Founded 1995 by Mr. Mariyala Vinod Kumar. Revenue had declined from ₹2L/month to ₹60-75K due to competition and COVID. Hand-written billing, no digital presence.
<img width="480" height="637" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-01 at 13 32 49" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/20c4d01c-a336-49bf-a251-64e572dc084c" />


## What I did
- Collected raw bill data Nov 2023–Jan 2024, entered into Google Sheets on-site
- Cleaned and categorised 8 service segments
- Sales analysis by day, category, and month
- RFM segmentation to classify customer cohorts
- Heatmapping across 4 KPIs to find bottlenecks
